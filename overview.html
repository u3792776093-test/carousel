Overview

Waterfront Living in One of Patmos’ Most Privileged Locations

Patmos Inn is a fully private seafront residence in Skala Patmos designed for travelers seeking quiet luxury, architectural authenticity, and direct connection to the sea.

Hosting up to six guests, the residence combines restored heritage details, handcrafted materials, and clean contemporary interiors naturally illuminated by uninterrupted Aegean light throughout the day.

Located directly above Saint Theologos Beach beside the marina, the property occupies one of the island’s rare true waterfront positions — a setting impossible to recreate today since the Skala’s coast line is totally built up.

Within a 2-minute walk, guests can reach:

  • Skala Port
  • Fine dining restaurants
  • Waterfront cafés
  • Boutique shopping
  • Daily conveniences

A car is not necessary during your stay.
