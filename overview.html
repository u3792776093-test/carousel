Waterfront Living in One of Patmos’ Most Privileged Locations

Patmos Inn is a fully private seafront residence in Skala Patmos designed for travelers seeking quiet luxury, architectural authenticity, and direct connection to the sea.

Hosting up to six guests, the residence combines restored heritage details and handcrafted materials with clean contemporary interiors filled with Aegean light.

Located directly above Saint Theologos Beach beside the marina, it is one of the island’s rare true waterfront homes.

A car is not necessary during your stay.

Heritage Architecture Restored for Contemporary Island Living

Originally built in 1920, Patmos Inn once guided ships safely into Skala Harbor.

Its distinctive double-framed tiled roof was designed for rainwater collection and island climate protection.

Today it has been carefully restored preserving stone walls, wooden openings, and traditional proportions.

Natural textures, soft materials, and sea-oriented interiors create an atmosphere that feels timeless, private, and deeply connected to Patmos.
