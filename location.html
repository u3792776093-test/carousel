Location

Walk Everywhere from the Waterfront

Patmos Inn is located directly on Saint Theologos Beach, steps from the 95 year A.D. Baptistery of Saint John the Theologian and the Marina, in the heart of Skala Patmos.

One of the few remaining true waterfront residences in central Skala, the property combines direct sea access, privacy, and immediate proximity to the island’s restaurants, boutiques, cafés, and port.

Guests experience life directly on the Aegean while remaining fully connected to the rhythm of the island.

Discover Patmos

Patmos is one of Greece’s most refined and discreet island destinations, known for its spiritual heritage, protected architecture, sophisticated atmosphere, and understated luxury.

Home to the UNESCO-listed Monastery of Saint John the Theologian and the Cave of the Apocalypse, the island attracts travelers seeking authenticity, culture, privacy, and a slower Mediterranean rhythm away from mass tourism.

Its combination of elegant simplicity, yacht culture, and preserved architectural identity has established Patmos as one of the Mediterranean’s most quietly exclusive destinations.
