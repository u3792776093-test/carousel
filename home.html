Quiet Waterfront Living on the Aegean

Patmos Inn is a fully private waterfront residence located directly above Saint Theologos Beach, steps from the 95 year A.D. Baptistery of Saint John the Theologian and the Marina, in the heart of Skala Patmos.

Carefully restored by its fourth-generation owner, the 100 sq. m. residence preserves its original 1920 architectural character while introducing refined contemporary comfort shaped around sea, light, and island rhythm.

Its privileged seafront position offers an increasingly rare way to experience Patmos: directly on the water, yet only moments from the island’s restaurants, boutiques, cafés, and port.

Wake to uninterrupted Aegean views, swim directly from the private terrace at sunrise, and spend evenings overlooking the illuminated Monastery of Saint John the Theologian in Chora.

Quiet, discreet, and deeply connected to the sea, Patmos Inn is designed for travelers who value privacy, location, and understated luxury above excess.
